Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich tomato sauce, savory ground meat, creamy ricotta or béchamel, and plenty of melted cheese. Baked to perfection, it comes out bubbling and golden on top, offering a hearty combination of flavors and textures. This comfort food is perfect for family dinners, special occasions, or simply when you’re craving something warm, filling, and delicious.
