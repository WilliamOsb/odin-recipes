Description

Spaghetti is a beloved Italian pasta made of long, thin strands that cook to a tender bite and pair beautifully with countless sauces. From bright tomato and basil to rich meat ragù, creamy carbonara, or simple garlic-and-oil, its shape holds flavor without feeling heavy. Twirled on a fork, it’s cozy and familiar, perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. Versatile and satisfying, spaghetti turns a few pantry staples into a comforting, crowd-pleasing meal.
