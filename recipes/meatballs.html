Description

Meatballs are a hearty and flavorful dish made by mixing ground meat with breadcrumbs, herbs, and seasonings, then shaping the mixture into bite-sized rounds. They can be baked, fried, or simmered in sauce, making them incredibly versatile across different cuisines. Often paired with spaghetti and marinara, tucked into a sub roll, or served on their own as appetizers, meatballs bring comfort and satisfaction to any meal. Their juicy texture and rich flavor make them a timeless favorite for family dinners and gatherings.
